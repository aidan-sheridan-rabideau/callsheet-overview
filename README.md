# CallSheet

**AI-powered call time prep for political campaigns.**

CallSheet streamlines the preparation and execution of political fundraising. We turn hours of weekly donor research, prospecting, and follow-ups into minutes of automated work.

🌐 **Product:** [callsheetwebsite.netlify.app](https://callsheetwebsite.netlify.app/)

---

## The Problem

The primary fundraising activity on any political campaign is call time — when a candidate personally calls donors to solicit contributions. For every hour of call time, staff spend roughly an hour preparing the candidate's call sheet: researching biographical details, donation history, and contact information for every donor.

When campaigns ramp to 40+ hours of call time per week, prep becomes unsustainable. Campaigns are forced to either burn money on additional staff or send candidates into calls with incomplete information.

## The Solution

Fundraising teams upload their donor lists. CallSheet orchestrates FEC and local contribution data, professional databases, and web scrapers, then uses LLMs to synthesize a complete brief on every donor — biographical background, donation history, and current contact info — in minutes.

Every call begins with the candidate fully prepped to maximize dollars raised.

## The Full Platform

Beyond preparation, CallSheet includes:

- **Call time execution** — manage live calls, log conversations, and track follow-ups
- **CRM sync** — all data flows back to the campaign's primary CRM
- **Donor prospecting** — identify new donors and expand the candidate's network

Prep, execution, and prospecting in one place.

## Why Now

LLMs are what make CallSheet viable. Synthesizing a usable donor profile from messy, multi-source data at this speed and quality wasn't technically possible 18 months ago.
